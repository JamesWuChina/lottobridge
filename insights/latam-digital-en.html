Latin America Digital Lottery Market Overview

Latin America, as a crucial component of the global lottery market, is experiencing unprecedented digital transformation. The region, with over 650 million inhabitants and continuously improving mobile internet penetration, provides vast opportunities for digital lottery development. The COVID-19 pandemic particularly accelerated the migration from traditional offline lottery purchasing to online platforms.

According to research data from LottoBridge Latin America Market Analysis Team, the LATAM digital lottery market reached $3.8 billion in 2024, with projections to grow to $6.1 billion in 2025, representing an annual growth rate exceeding 60%. Mexico, Brazil, and Argentina, the three core markets, account for 75% of the entire regional market share.
