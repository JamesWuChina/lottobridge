Thermal Technologies

Our thermal printing technology delivers superior print quality, speed, and reliability for lottery ticket production, supporting both direct thermal and thermal transfer methods.

Direct Thermal

High-speed direct thermal printing for instant tickets and receipts with no ribbon required, offering cost-effective operation.

  • No ink or ribbon required
  • Fast printing speeds up to 8 inches/second
  • Cost-effective operation
  • Ideal for short-term applications
  • Minimal maintenance required
  • Compact printer design

Thermal Transfer

Superior print quality with thermal transfer technology, providing durable, fade-resistant lottery tickets for long-term use.

  • Exceptional print quality and durability
  • Fade and scratch resistant
  • Wide media compatibility
  • Long-lasting print results
  • Chemical resistance
  • Professional appearance
